Trust Architecture
Six Verifiable Pillars
Creating the most certain trust in an uncertain world, realized through engineering and governance.
Funds Verifiable
Hot-cold wallet separation + multi-sig/MPC + multi-layer withdrawal risk controls; explore Proof of Reserves (PoR) and liability disclosures.
Trades Verifiable
Matching logs and risk rules recorded, auditable and traceable.
Prices Verifiable
Multi-source indices, outlier removal, anomaly circuit breakers, and regular disclosure of index composition.
Fairness Verifiable
Transparent funding rate methodology, historical back-testing, anti-manipulation monitoring and disclosure of results.
Compliance Verifiable
KYC/AML, privacy and data governance, aligned with external audits and certifications.
Openness Verifiable
Weekly security reports and co-created white papers with internal and external experts, raising the trust ceiling of the industry.
